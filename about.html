Professional Print Preparation Made Simple

Welcome to PrintSizeChecker.com. We provide an industry-leading, browser-based solution for photographers, digital artists, and designers who need to ensure their digital files translate perfectly into physical prints.

The Mission Behind the Tool

In a world of high-resolution displays, it’s easy to forget that pixels (PPI) and dots (DPI) operate differently. Many incredible photos are ruined at the printing stage because of incorrect resolution or improper cropping. Our mission is to bridge the gap between your digital screen and the final print lab.

“Our goal is to eliminate the 'trial and error' of photo printing. By providing instant feedback on image density and resolution, we save our users time, money, and wasted paper.”

Why Use PrintSizeChecker?

Standard image viewers don't tell you how a 3000px image will look on a 24x36 canvas. We do. Our tool calculates the effective PPI (Pixels Per Inch) based on your desired print dimensions, warning you before you send a pixelated file to the lab.

Key Features:

  • Real-time Quality Tiers: Instantly see if your file is "Pro Quality," "Good," or "Risky."
  • Smart Cropping: Visualize exactly how your image fits standard frame ratios (4:5, 2:3, 1:1, etc.).
  • Privacy First: Unlike other converters, your images are never uploaded to a server. All processing happens locally in your browser's memory using JavaScript.

Built for the Creative Community

Whether you are preparing a gallery exhibition or just printing family photos for the living room, PrintSizeChecker offers professional-grade precision without the Adobe Creative Cloud price tag. We are proud to support photographers worldwide by keeping this tool 100% free and accessible.
